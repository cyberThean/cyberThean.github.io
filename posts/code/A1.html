    What is that?

    a robots.txt archive is used to disallow access for certain bots and agents to your page. Bots and agent, also called spiders, are used to list webpages and used for browsers like google or bing. Many spam bots do not take care (rude) of this archives, but google or bing, for example do. So, here i let you guys, A ROBOTS.TXT GENERATOR for Linux.

    It is a .sh, so to make it work use:

    chmod u+x robotsgenerator.sh

    ./robotsgenerator.sh

    You can edit it afterwards, or see the next example:

    #basic robot User-agent: * Disallow: /wp/ Disallow: /ir/ Disallow: /google-no/ Disallow: /locateip.php Disallow: /get_queries.php Disallow: /poll/ Disallow: /usearch/ Disallow: /advert/ Disallow: /comments/ Disallow: /captcha/ http://https://YOURF*CKINGSITEHERE.com/sitemap.xml
